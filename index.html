Why I Built This QR Code Generator

I was tired of so-called "free" QR code generators that create dynamic links and track every scan — or worse, deactivate your code unless you buy a subscription. So I built my own tool: a free, static QR code generator that works forever without middlemen, cookies, tracking, or login requirements. I wanted something simple, private, and reliable — because even the QR codes on many professional business cards expire, which hurts brands in the long run. This generator is my way of giving everyone direct, lasting access to a truly free QR code generator that just works.
